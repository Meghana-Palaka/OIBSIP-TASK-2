MOTHER TERESA

Saint Teresa of Calcutta

What is Mother Teresa known for?

Mother Teresa is known for her missionary work in helping the poor. She was an empathetic devout catholic nun whose mission was to lessen the mystery that sick and poor went through. She is considered to be the greatest humanitarian to have ever lived in the 20th century.

What organizatons was Mother Teresa associated with?

"The Order of the Missionaries of Charity", an organization founded (1948) by Mother Teresa (recipient of the Nobel Peace Prize of 1979), cares for the blind, the elderly, the dying, and people afflicted with leprosy in the poorest sections of the city. There are several medical colleges, in addition to other medical research centres.
